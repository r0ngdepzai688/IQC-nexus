# 00. Overview: New Models

## Purpose of New Models
The New Models module is designed to orchestrate and track the introduction of new products into mass production. It ensures that all quality, supplier, and manufacturing readiness prerequisites are met before mass production begins.

## Why this module exists
To replace disjointed spreadsheets and manual tracking with a unified, AI-powered system that prevents delays, highlights risks early, and guarantees quality standards are maintained from the design phase to mass production.

## Business Goals
1. Ensure 100% material and vendor readiness prior to Mass Production (MP).
2. Minimize risks and delays during product launch.
3. Standardize and automate the tracking of DFx, BOM, and Vendor Capabilities.
4. Provide a single source of truth for all New Model quality data.

## Success Criteria
- Zero critical open issues at Mass Production (MP) stage.
- 100% completion of Golden Samples and Inspection Specs before MP.
- Automated KPI calculation and real-time dashboard visibility.
- Seamless escalation of high risks.

## Module Boundaries
The New Models module focuses on the lifecycle from Engineering Validation (DV) through to Mass Production (MP), specifically focusing on IQC's involvement from PV (Pilot Validation) onward.

## Relationship with Other Modules
- **Workforce**: Interacts to assign tasks based on roles (Cell Leaders, Engineers).
- **Inspection**: Provides the Inspection Specs (ISS) and tracks their creation and approval.
- **Compliance**: Ensures Vendor capability and CPK data meet required regulatory and internal standards.
- **Support**: Relies on Support for notification delivery, escalation routing, and access to knowledge bases.
