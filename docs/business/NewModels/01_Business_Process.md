# 01. Business Process

## The Complete IQC Business Process

The product lifecycle encompasses several stages:
**DV (Design Validation) ↓ PV (Pilot Validation) ↓ PR (Production Readiness) ↓ SR (System Readiness) ↓ MP (Mass Production)**

*Note: IQC starts actively working at the **PV** stage.*

![IQC Business Process](../assets/process.png)

## Chronological Activities

1. **DV (Design Validation)**
   - Preliminary design evaluation (R&D focus).
   - Early visibility for IQC but no active blocking actions.

2. **PV (Pilot Validation)**
   - **Receive Model Plans:** HQ provides model list, launch schedule, BOM, Vendor List, and initial Risk Information.
   - **DFx Collection:** Collect Design for Excellence (DFx) and Risk Factors. Send to HQ and track replies (Pending -> Reviewed -> Completed).
   - **BOM Analysis:** Download BOM, filter direct materials, generate IQC material list, and map categories and suppliers.
   - **Grade A Material Handling:** Compare Grade A material list with BOM and determine purchased materials.
   - **Vendor Capability Request:** Collect CPK, Capability, Yield, and Performance data from vendors.
   - **Approval Tracking:** Track code approval, Inspection Spec (ISS) creation, drawing release, and material approval.
   - **Golden Sample Tracking:** Track golden samples, limit samples, color samples, storage locations, and approval statuses.
   - **Vendor Verification:** Random verification, capability reviews, gap analysis, and issue list compilation.

3. **PR (Production Readiness)**
   - Track incoming lots and IQC results.
   - Evaluate Grade A materials.
   - Collect Mass Production Pilot Run (MPPR) data.
   - Prepare Executive Report: Risk Summary and Final Readiness assessment.

4. **SR (System Readiness)**
   - Track Mass Production Readiness.
   - Monitor and resolve Supplier Issues.
   - Address all Open Issues.
   - Evaluate and mitigate Remaining Risks.

5. **MP (Mass Production)**
   - Continuous monitoring via KPIs.
   - Track Yield and Issue Trends.
   - Evaluate Supplier Performance.
   - Maintain overall Mass Production Health.
