# 09. Risk Management

The New Models module proactively tracks and escalates risks to prevent delays to Mass Production.

## Risk Classification

- **Low:** Minor issue, easily resolvable by the assigned Staff within regular workflow. Does not impact timeline.
- **Medium:** Issue requiring Part Leader intervention. Potential slight delay to specific tasks, but overall phase timeline is safe.
- **High:** Significant issue requiring Group Leader attention. High probability of delaying the current phase (PV/PR/SR).
- **Critical:** Blocker issue. Team Leader must be notified immediately. Threatens the overall MP schedule or critical quality standards.

## Escalation Rules

- **Low/Medium Risks:** Remain with the assigned Owner.
- **High Risks:** Auto-escalate to Group Leader if not mitigated within 3 days.
- **Critical Risks:** Instantly notify Team Leader upon creation. Daily status update required until downgraded.

## Lifecycle Management

- **Owner:** Every risk must have a single responsible Assignee.
- **Deadline:** Every risk must have a target resolution date.
- **Mitigation:** A documented action plan is required for all High and Critical risks.
- **Closure:** Risks can only be closed when the mitigation plan is completed and verified by the Assignee's manager.
